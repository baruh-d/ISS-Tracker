Random Fun Facts

  • Astronauts' average salary: $80,000 per year.
  • The ISS orbits the Earth approximately every 90 minutes.
  • The ISS travels at a speed of about 28,000 kilometers (17,500 miles) per hour.
  • The ISS is as large as a football field.
  • Astronauts aboard the ISS experience 16 sunrises and sunsets in a 24-hour period.
  • The space station has a weight of over 400,000 kilograms (approximately 440 tons).
  • It takes only 90 minutes for the ISS to complete one orbit around the Earth.
  • To maintain their health, astronauts exercise for about two hours each day to counteract muscle and bone loss in microgravity.
  • The ISS has a sophisticated water recovery system that recycles urine and moisture from the air into clean drinking water.
  • The ISS has been continuously occupied since November 2000, demonstrating international cooperation in space exploration.
  • Astronauts aboard the ISS communicate with Mission Control on Earth using a system of satellites.
  • The station's solar panels cover an area equivalent to a football field to generate electricity from sunlight.
  • The station has onboard laboratories for scientific research in areas such as biology, physics, and astronomy.
  • Astronauts on the ISS experience microgravity, which means they can float and move in any direction inside the station.
